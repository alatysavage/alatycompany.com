<h1>ingredients</h1>
<ul>
<li> 1 cup butter of margarine,softened</li>
<li> 3/4 cup sugar</li>
<li> 3/4 cup Brown sugar,packed</li>
<li> 2 Eggs,large</li>
<li> 2 Teaspoons Vanilla</li>
<li> 2 1/4 cups flour,unsifted</li>
<li> 1 teaspoon Baking soda</li>
<li> 1/2 teaspoon salt</li>
<li> 1 cup Walnuts or pecans,chopped(optional)</li>
<li> 2 cups KCC Bittersweet 60% CaCao Baking Chips</li>
</ul>

<h1> Baking instructions </h1>
<ul>
<li> Heat oven to 500 degrees</li>
<li> stir flour with baking soda and salt;set aside.</li>
<li>in large mixing bowl,beat butter with sugar,and brown sugar
 at medium speed until creamy and ightended in color</li>
</ul>
